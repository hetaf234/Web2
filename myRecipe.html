Margherita Pizza
  • Flour — 2 cups
  • Yeast — 1 tsp
  • Water — 3/4 cup
  • Tomato sauce — 1/2 cup
  • Mozzarella — 200 g
  • Basil — 6 leaves
  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Spread the sauce.
  3. Add mozzarella.
  4. Bake until done.
  5. Garnish with basil.

Spaghetti Carbonara
  • Spaghetti — 200 g
  • Eggs — 2 large
  • Parmesan — 50 g
  • Beef bacon — 100 g
  • Black pepper — 1/2 tsp
  • Salt — to taste
  1. Boil the pasta.
  2. Cook beef bacon until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs with cheese.
  4. Combine pasta with sauce gently.
  5. Season with black pepper.

Lasagna
  • Lasagna sheets — 9 sheets
  • Minced meat — 300 g
  • Tomato sauce — 1 cup
  • Béchamel sauce — 1 cup
  • Mozzarella — 200 g
  • Olive oil — 1 tbsp
  1. Cook minced meat with tomato sauce.
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, and béchamel.
  3. Repeat layers.
  4. Top with mozzarella.
  5. Bake until golden.
